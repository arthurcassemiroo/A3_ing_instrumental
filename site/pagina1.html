The acronym SDGs, which stands for Sustainable Development Goals, is part of the so-called "Agenda 2030". It is a global pact signed during the United Nations Summit in 2015 by 193 member countries. The agenda consists of 17 ambitious and interconnected goals, unfolded into 169 targets, with a focus on overcoming the main development challenges faced by people in Brazil and around the world, promoting global sustainable growth by 2030.

About the SDG's

  • THE 17

    Sustainable Development Goals encompass various themes relating to environmental and social aspects.

  • THE FOCUS

    End poverty, protect the environment and climate, and ensure that people everywhere can enjoy peace and prosperity.

  • THE ACTION PLAN

    Each SDG is divided into targets, making it clearer the actions that each country needs to take to achieve sustainable living.

In the following section, we will discuss and elaborate on the following SDGs and how technology can help achieve these goals:

Zero Hunger and Sustainable Agriculture: End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

Information technology can be used to improve data collection and sharing related to agriculture, climate, and soil, enabling more efficient management of agricultural resources and better crop forecasting. Precision agriculture, which involves the use of sensors, drones, and geographic information systems, can help optimize the use of water, fertilizers, and pesticides, reducing waste and increasing farmers' productivity.

Clean Water and Sanitation: Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.


Advanced water treatment technologies, such as reverse osmosis and ultraviolet disinfection, can help purify water and make it safe for human consumption. Sensors and monitoring systems can assist in the detection of pollutants and efficient management of water resources, enabling a quick response to water scarcity situations. Sustainable sanitation solutions, such as advanced toilet technologies, can help improve access to appropriate sanitation facilities.

Sustainable Cities and Communities: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.


Smart building technology can be used to create energy-efficient and sustainable buildings, reducing energy consumption and carbon emissions. Smart transportation solutions, such as electric vehicles and car-sharing systems, can reduce congestion and greenhouse gas emissions in cities. Digital platforms can facilitate citizen participation in urban decision-making, allowing residents to contribute ideas and opinions on sustainable community development.

Climate Action: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.


Renewable energy technology, such as solar and wind power, can provide clean and sustainable sources of energy, reducing dependence on fossil fuels and decreasing greenhouse gas emissions. Climate monitoring and disaster detection solutions can help predict and mitigate the impacts of climate change, enabling a more effective response to extreme events. Carbon capture and storage technology can help reduce carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere, contributing to climate stabilization.

Life Below Water: Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development.


Ocean monitoring technology can help identify areas of overfishing, pollution, and degradation of marine ecosystems, enabling more efficient conservation and management measures. Underwater drones and imaging systems can assist in research and monitoring of marine species, helping to better understand marine ecosystems and identify areas for protection. Coastal water purification solutions and artificial reefs can help restore damaged marine ecosystems and promote coastal biodiversity.

The Sustainable Development Goals are a roadmap towards a better and more sustainable world. By working together and taking action, we can address poverty, inequality, climate change, and protect our planet. Let us strive to achieve the SDGs to ensure a brighter future for all!
